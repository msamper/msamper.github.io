About me

I am a second year Computer Science student at the University of Florida. My interests include Web Development, Game Design, Cultural Awareness, Brotherhood, and more.

I currently work at nexGEN at the University of Florida as a Software Development intern, working on projects both internal and external. One of my current projects is aimed at increasing millenial employee retention through the creation of an analytics software.

When I'm not working on code, I spend my time educating others and sharing my culture. I work as an ambassador for Hispanic-Latino Affairs at UF and I help put on meaningful programming such as Spanish/Portuguese Conversation to help educate students and truly make UF a home away from home for the minority student population.
